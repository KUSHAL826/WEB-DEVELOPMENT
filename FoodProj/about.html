About Us

We are a home-based food powder preparation and supply unit dedicated to providing pure, hygienic, and traditionally prepared spice powders to our local community. All our products are prepared at home in small batches using traditional methods.

We carefully select high-quality raw ingredients, clean them thoroughly, and follow natural drying processes to preserve flavour, aroma, and nutritional value. No artificial colours, preservatives, or chemicals are added at any stage.

Each batch is prepared with personal care and attention to ensure freshness and consistent taste. Since production is limited, customers always receive freshly prepared products rather than mass-produced items.

Our focus is on quality over quantity. We believe good food starts with honest ingredients and clean preparation, making our powders suitable for everyday cooking and traditional recipes.

Currently, we supply only within Kallembella Hobli, Sira Taluk, Tumakuru District, allowing us to maintain freshness and direct customer trust. More homemade products will be introduced in the future based on demand.
